CE marking of machinery,

The conformity work produces documents that all have to agree with one another: the risk assessment, the applicable essential requirements, the standards applied, the measures taken. openconformity keeps that work as one connected model, and generates the documents from it.

How it works

1

Describe the machine and its hazards

Break the product down into its parts, and record the hazards each one presents. Every entity is entered once and referred to everywhere else.

2

Connect them to the law and the standards

Link the hazards to the essential health and safety requirements that apply, and to the clauses of the standards that address them.

3

Generate the artefacts

Hazard lists, requirement specifications, and traceability come out as views of the model, ready to go into your own technical file.

What it is

Free and open-source

Licensed under the EUPL-1.2. The source, the specification, and the decision log are public.

Runs in the browser

Nothing to install, update, or maintain. Open a URL and work.

Your data stays yours

No account and no server. A project is a single file on your own machine.

Machinery Regulation

Built for CE marking of machinery under Regulation (EU) 2023/1230.

Everything exists once

A hazard is entered a single time, and every artefact that mentions it reads the same entry.

Traceable in both directions

From a measure back to the hazard it addresses, and from a requirement forward to what satisfies it.

The model

Entities are typed, and so are the relationships between them. A relationship carries meaning, which is what makes a chain like this readable in both directions, and what lets a change at one end surface at the other.
